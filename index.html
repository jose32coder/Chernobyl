April 26, 1986. Following an explosion at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant, workers at the plant surveyed the damage. When reports show that the reactor core "disappeared", the chief engineer, Anatoly Dyatlov, launches an investigation. Academician Valery Legasov is assigned to the Chernobyl special force.
